Society of Justice Warriors

The Society of Justice Warriors is sponsored by Caper in accordance with our reformed policies and values. Despite certain controversies and allegations regarding our partners, the CCA, Caper is working to make crime-fighting inclusive and non-discriminatory based on personal characteristics such as race, ethnicity, sex, age, sexual orientation, and gender identity, among others. Tomorrow's heroes are more diverse than ever, and they're streaming today!

Signature Look: Pink Skin, Blue/Yellow Costume (Pan Flag)

Affiliations: Leader of Society of Justice Warriors, Caper

Abilities: Induced Hallucinogenic Euphoria

Profile: Found in the woods as a child and raised by travelling Romani, Pan can only speculate about his origins and abilities. Their sponsorship has already paid off, as he has turned out to be an excellent leader for the SJWs and a talented entertainer on his livestreams every Monday, Wednesday, and Friday at 8:00 PM here on Caper!

Identity:(She/Her), Variant, United States

Generation: Silicon

Signature Look: Blue/Pink/White

Affiliations: Society of Justice Warriors, Caper

Abilities: Portal Generation, Pocket Dimension Storage

Profile: Alisha Wright was a promising young track and field athlete and straight-A student whose life was tragically changed on the day that her variant abilities manifested. She was in class when portals began to appear around her, transporting her classmates and much of the classroom to a then-unknown location. Initially she was unable to control these powers, and indeed was not immediately aware that she was the source of the occurence. Unfortunately the police were called by her teacher, and they quickly deduced that she was the variant responsible. In the ensuing panic, one of the officers fired upon her, resulting in a spinal injury which would render her paraplegic. While recovering from her wounds in the hospital, she was able to take better control of her abilities, and safely returned everyone from the pocket dimension they'd been stored in. Learn more in her livestreams every Tuesday at 8:00 PM here on Caper!
