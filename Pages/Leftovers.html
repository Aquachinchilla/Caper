Celestia

Photo courtesy of Kestral, photographer for the Halcyon Herald

  • Real Name: Alicia Grant
  • CCA: No
  • Identity:(She/Her), Human, United States
  • Generation: Silicon
  • Signature Look: Translucent blue skin, Black/White, hooded
  • Affiliations: Daughter of Dr. Gregory Grant; Best friend of Gale (Blue Eagle Scouts)
  • Abilities: Density Manipulation
  • Profile: First and only successful recipient of the disgraced Dr. Grant's experimental program for creating superhumans. Empowered by unknown cosmic rays with extraordinarily potent manipulation of molecular density. Known to be capable of creating micro-black holes, vaporizing objects, and limited phasing. Celestia's difficulty controlling her own overwhelming power has worked against her in the past, but her winning personality and work ethic are sure to make her a major player some day.
