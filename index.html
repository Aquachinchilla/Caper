Here at Caper, our priority is your safety! That's why we've created a revolutionary new app that lets average users like you interact with real CCA licensed superheroes!

In an emergency and need help right now? Click the big Red Exclamation Mark and you'll be saved with just the push of a button!

We Also offer a real time CrimeMap to monitor and avoid any criminal activity or super-powered conflicts! No more finding out halfway through your daily commute that the Gorgomoth is throwing cars at the Exemplars again!

But Caper is so much more than just a safety tool! We also offer community updated Parahuman Profiles so you can see the latest up to date information on Halcyon City's own heroes and rogues!

Don't forget our CaperChat functionality and SuperForums for all your super-powered socializing needs!
